# Privacy Policy for SnapCal

**Last Updated: April 9, 2026**

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to SnapCal. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect
*   **Health and Fitness Data:** We collect information you provide such as your height, weight, age, gender, activity level, and calorie goals to calculate your personalized nutrition plan.
*   **Images:** When you use the "Snap" feature, we receive images of your food to provide nutritional analysis.
*   **Account Data:** If you create an account, we collect your email address and display name.
*   **Usage Data:** We collect basic information about how you interact with the app for stability and performance monitoring.

## 3. How We Use Your Information
*   To provide and maintain our Service, including calculating your nutritional targets.
*   To analyze food images using Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide caloric estimates.
*   To personalize your experience and provide an "AI Coach" fitness assistant.
*   To manage your account and subscription.

## 4. Data Processing and AI Providers
To provide the food analysis feature, your images and meal descriptions are processed by third-party AI service providers (Google Gemini and Groq). These providers receive data only for the purpose of analysis and do not use it to identify you personally. **We do not sell your data to third parties.**

## 5. Data Retention
We retain your health and meal logs as long as your account is active to provide you with historical reports. You can delete your logs or your account at any time within the app settings.

## 6. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your sensitive health data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 7. Children's Privacy
SnapCal is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information directly within the app.

## 9. Data Deletion
We provide users with the ability to delete their account and associated data at any time.
*   **In-App Deletion:** You can delete your account permanently by going to **Settings > Account > Delete Account**. This will immediately and permanently remove your profile, health metrics, and meal logs from our active databases.
*   **Online Request:** If you are unable to access the app and wish to request account deletion, please contact us at our support email. We will process your request within 30 days.
